About Us

Atlas Switch is the largest WBE (Women's business Enterprise) manufacturer of electrical service and distribution equipment in New York. We serve residential, commercial and industrial buildings throughout New York State. Through some of our excellent partners, we have expanded to parts of New Jersey, Connecticut, Pennsylvania and Massachusets. We can custom manufacture panel boards and switchboards to project specific requirements and building specific space constraints. We provide further support for the service and distribution packages by supplying transformers, disconnect switches, busway and metering solutions sourced from our relationships with other manufacturers.

We have worked for NYC Port Authority, Mount Sinai Health System, New York University, Northwell Health, JP Morgan Chase, Citibank, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Bloomberg and many more. We have been fortunate to have been a part of some major development projects, including the rebuilding of World Trace Center at Tower 4, Yankee Stadium, Madison Square Guarden, the rebuilding of TWA hotel at JFk, the Hudson Yards Development projects, and we look forward to being involved in shaping the future of New York City's iconic skyline.
